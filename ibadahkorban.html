Ibadah Korban

The annual ibadah korban, or Hari Raya Haji, is a joyous and solemn occasion in Malaysia. It is a time for Muslims to commemorate the Prophet Ibrahim's willingness to sacrifice his son Ishmael as an act of obedience to God. In Malaysia, the ibadah korban is celebrated on the 10th day of Zulhijjah, the month of the Hajj pilgrimage. On the morning of Eid al-Adha, Muslims gather at mosques or open-air prayer grounds to perform the Eid prayers. After the prayers, they proceed to slaughter their sacrificial animals, which are usually sheep, goats, or cows.
