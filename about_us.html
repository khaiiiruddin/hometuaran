About Us

Home Tuaran is a home for orphans that managed by Excellence. Home Tuaran is a home for orphans and the poor that accommodates a total of 70 orphans and the poor (50 Non Resident and 20 Resident) located in the Tuaran district, in the area of ​​Kg, Bolong Tuaran. Home Tuaran is managed by the Persatuan Pengurusan Home Tuaran.

Vision

Home Tuaran is to be an institution that cares for orphans and the needy towards the well-being of the world and the well-being of the hereafter

Mission

Home Tuaran is to work together to protect and educate orphans and orphans for God's sake
